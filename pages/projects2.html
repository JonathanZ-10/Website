This website was my first ever project dealing with web development and design.
It required meticulous planning and designing, although no amount of planning
could have prepared me for the complications of designing an entire website.

Angular, or any other libraries that could have made the project less tidious.
Ultimately the experienced taught me the intricacies of design and how it
can come together with code to make a website come to life.
